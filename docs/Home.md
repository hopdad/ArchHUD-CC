# ArchHUD

A feature-rich flight HUD for Dual Universe with full autopilot, altitude hold, orbital mechanics, radar, shields, AGG, damage monitoring, and extensive customization.

> **WARNING: DO NOT USE HUDS WITH IN GAME GRAPHICS VOXEL RENDERING SETTING OF AUTO OR MAX NUMBER OF THREADS**

---

## Quick Start

1. Download `ArchHUD.zip` from the [Releases](../../releases) page.
2. Extract it into your `autoconf/custom` folder (see [Installation](Installation) for paths).
3. In-game: right-click your seat > **Advanced** > **Update custom autoconflist** > **Run custom autoconfigure** > select **ArchHud**.

---

## Feature Highlights

- **Full interplanetary autopilot** -- ground to ground, planet to planet
- **Altitude hold**, orbital establishment, low-orbit hops
- **Brake landing** with safety feedback
- **Glide and Parachute re-entry** with pilot-selectable mode (`/reentry` command)
- **Graduated collision avoidance** -- 3-tier: caution / warning / emergency
- **Vertical takeoff mode**
- **Radar** -- collision detection, abandoned contacts, friendlies
- **Shield management** with auto-resist
- **AGG support** with waypoint-saved heights
- **Damage monitoring** with repair arrows
- **Emergency warp**
- **Follow mode** (remote control)
- **ECU support** with full HUD
- **Customizable UI** -- colors, positions, resolution scaling
- **Sound pack support**
- **Multipoint route support**
- **Programming board add-ons** -- Telemetry Dashboard, Radar Display, Damage Display, Flight Recorder, Route Planner
- **Human-readable status codes** for all flight modes
- **Pre-flight validation** -- thrust-to-weight, radar checks
- **Speed assist** with visible throttle limiting
